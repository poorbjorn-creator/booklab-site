The Stoicism Starter Pack

Stoicism is the most practical philosophy I've ever come across. No hand-wavy abstractions — just clear thinking about how to live well, handle adversity, and find peace of mind. These are the books that got me into Stoicism and the ones I keep coming back to.

01
A Guide to the Good Life: The Ancient Art of Stoic Joy
William B. Irvine
Philosophy

One of my all-time favorites — a book I've reread countless times. A great introduction to Stoic philosophy and a practical guide for implementing stoic practices in modern life to gain peace of mind.

Read if:
  • You're looking for a philosophy of life
  • You want practical tips for psychological well-being
Buy on Amazon →
02
Meditations
Marcus Aurelius
Philosophy

The philosopher king's own writings about his Stoicism. The most accessible ancient classic you'll find.

Read if:
  • You want a practical philosophy of life
  • You want to read an accessible ancient classic
Buy on Amazon →
03
On the Shortness of Life
Seneca
Essay

A short essay on the duration of life — is it too short, or have we been given enough? As relevant today as it was 2000 years ago.

Read if:
  • You want to get your priorities straight
  • You tend to let others dictate how you spend your time
Buy on Amazon →
04
The Daily Stoic
Ryan Holiday
Philosophy / Self-Help

366 meditations on wisdom, perseverance, and the art of living. One quote and reflection per day. Perfect for building a daily Stoic practice.

Read if:
  • You want a daily philosophical practice
  • You prefer bite-sized wisdom over long reads
Buy on Amazon →
05
Stillness Is the Key
Ryan Holiday
Philosophy / Self-Help

How the great thinkers, artists, and leaders throughout history found stillness amidst chaos. Draws from Stoic, Buddhist, and other traditions.

Read if:
  • You feel overwhelmed by modern life
  • You want to cultivate calm and focus
Buy on Amazon →
06
Letters from a Stoic
Seneca
Philosophy

Seneca's personal letters to his friend Lucilius — covering everything from grief to the good life. More intimate and practical than most philosophy books.

Read if:
  • You enjoyed On the Shortness of Life and want more Seneca
  • You like philosophy delivered as personal advice
Buy on Amazon →
